Ice Cubes

Try this delicious frozen treat on a hot summer day!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of water (approximately)
  • 2 tablespoons of water (additional if needed)

Directions

  1. Take ice trays over to the sink and fill them with cold water. (Hot water will freeze faster and more clear).
  2. Place the water filled ice trays into the freezer.
  3. Shut the door to the freezer.
  4. Be sure to leave for around 4-6 hours at least to make sure it is frozen.
