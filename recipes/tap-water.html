Tap Water

Description

A low-calorie treat with minimal preparation time. Cleanup is as easy as having paper towels on hand.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of water
  • 1 teaspoon of water

Directions

  1. Place glass under tap.
  2. Turn tap on.
  3. Fill glass with water almost to top.
  4. Turn tap off.
  5. Top up glass with additional teaspoons of water until full.
  6. Use paper towel to mop up any spillage.
  7. Enjoy!
